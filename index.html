When Matthew picked up his first WRX and asked around for the right shop, every Subaru owner in Southern California pointed him to one address in Huntington Beach. That's how Powered By RPM works.

RPM has been building performance cars in Huntington Beach for over a decade. The team texts photo updates as the job progresses. They tell customers exactly what's wrong and exactly what it costs. And they treat every car like it's the only one in the shop — whether it's a daily-driver Crosstrek or a 500-horsepower track build.

No upsells. No shortcuts. Just the kind of work that earns a reputation customers will drive 30+ miles to keep.

Today, RPM is the local Authorized Install Facility for Crawford Performance, a trusted partner of ACT, ETS, APR, and Koyorad, and the shop your friend with the loud Subaru already told you about.
