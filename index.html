04 — Frequently Asked

The honest answers.

Do you work on cars besides Subaru?

Our specialty is the Subaru, BRZ, and 86 platform. We take on select work for other AWD performance cars — call to discuss your project.

How long does a typical build take?

A bolt-on package and tune is usually 1–2 weeks. A full engine build with fabrication is 6–10 weeks depending on parts availability and complexity.

What's the minimum project you'll take?

We do everything from a basic clutch install through full motor builds. There's no minimum — call us for an honest assessment.

Do you offer financing?

We work with several aftermarket financing partners. Talk to the team about options that fit your build.

Can I ship my car to you?

Yes — we regularly take in builds from out of state. We'll coordinate transport and handle the entire build remotely with documented updates.

02 — Before You Drop By

A few notes.

Do I need an appointment?

For consultations and minor work, walk-ins are welcome during business hours. For builds and tunes, we book by appointment to give your car proper attention.

Where do I park?

The shop has dedicated parking out front of Unit I. If lot is full, street parking on Redondo Cir is available.

Can I watch my car being worked on?

Yes — we encourage it. We'll show you what's coming apart, walk you through the install, and explain anything you're unsure about.

What payment methods do you take?

Card, Zelle, ACH, cash, and aftermarket financing through select partners. Call to discuss.
